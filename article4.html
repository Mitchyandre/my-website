Sleep Better Tonight: Tips for Quality Rest

Importance of Sleep

Good sleep is essential for a healthy body and mind. Here are tips to help you improve your sleep:

Sleep Tips

  • Stick to a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine.
  • Make your bedroom comfortable and quiet.
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bed.
  • Limit screen time an hour before sleeping.

By improving your sleep habits, you can wake up feeling refreshed and energized.
